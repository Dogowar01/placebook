📍 Stamped

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Stamped does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything you add to the app stays on your device.

What data Stamped stores

All data you enter — places, notes, photos, trips, food logs, and settings — is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export a backup file yourself.

Third-party services

Stamped uses the following external services to power certain features. Each service receives only the minimum information needed to function:

  • OpenFreeMap / MapLibre — provides the map tiles you see on screen. Your approximate map view location is sent to their servers to load the correct tiles. No personal data is attached. OpenFreeMap privacy policy.
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) — used to convert an address into map coordinates when you paste an event from your clipboard. Only the address text you provide is sent. OpenStreetMap privacy policy.
  • Open-Meteo — provides the weather widget on the map screen. Your current map centre coordinates are sent to fetch local weather. No personal data is attached. Open-Meteo terms.
  • Oura Ring API (optional) — if you choose to connect your Oura Ring, your personal access token is stored on your device only. Health data is fetched directly from Oura's servers and cached locally. We never see or store this data. Oura privacy policy.

Photos

Photos you add to your places are stored entirely on your device using IndexedDB. They are never uploaded to any server.

Location

If you grant location permission, your device's GPS coordinates are used only to centre the map and suggest where to place a new pin. Your location is not stored on any server.

Analytics & advertising

Stamped contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash reporting of any kind.

Children

Stamped does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. Since no data is collected at all, the app is suitable for all ages.

Data backup & deletion

You can export all your data at any time from the Passport → Backup tab. To delete all your data, clear the app's data through your device settings or remove the app from your home screen.

iPhone users: removing Stamped from your home screen on iOS permanently deletes all locally stored data. Export a backup first if you want to keep your places.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect it. Significant changes will be noted in the app's update release notes.
